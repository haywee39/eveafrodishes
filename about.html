About Eve Afro Dishes

Eve Afro Dishes is a culinary haven in the UK, bringing the authentic taste of Nigerian cuisine to your table.
We take pride in serving traditional African dishes that celebrate culture, comfort, and community.

Our menu features a rich variety — from flavorful stews and beans dishes to soups, rice, yam specialties, starters, extras, and signature specials — all prepared with passion and originality.
Every plate is crafted to deliver not just a meal, but a true taste of home.

taste you never forget ...

At Eve Afro Dishes, food is more than a meal — it’s an experience.
Our slogan, “Taste you never forget…”, embodies the promise that every bite leaves a lasting impression.

Each dish is a fusion of authentic flavors and culinary passion, ensuring that once you taste it, the memory lingers, inviting you back for more.
